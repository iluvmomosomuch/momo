Momo

Momo are bite-size dumplings made with a spoonful of stuffing wrapped in dough. Momo are usually steamed, though they are sometimes fried or steam-fried. Meat or vegetables fillings becomes succulent as it produces an intensively flavored broth sealed inside the wrappers

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Chicken
  • vegetables
  • Steamer
  • Flour
  • Oil
  • Cups or anything small and round
  • Tomatoes if you want chutney

RECIPE

  1. Make the stuffing
  2. Knead the dough (using flour)
  3. Use your cups or other items to make small circles in the dough
  4. Oil the steamer
  5. Add the stuffing on the Dough then turn it into a nice shape
  6. Carefully place them into the steamer one by one and make sure they are not to close
  7. Start steaming it for 20 minutes
  8. After steaming take it out and enjoy!
  9. Best served with famous momo chutney
